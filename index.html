AM I BROKE??

AM I BROKE is a simple tool to help you determine if you are financially broke or not. It takes into account your income, expenses, and savings to give you a clear picture of your financial situation.

To use AM I BROKE, simply enter your monthly income, monthly expenses, and total savings. The tool will then calculate your net worth and provide you with an answer to the question "Am I broke?"

Remember, being broke is not just about how much money you have, but also about how you manage it. AM I BROKE can help you understand your financial health and make informed decisions about your money.
